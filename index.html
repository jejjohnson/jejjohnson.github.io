Biography

Juan Emmanuel Johnson is current a PhD candidate at the university of Valencia. He is currently working on using kernel methods and dependence estimation with applications in remote sensing for Earth applications. He has experience in using semisupervised kernel methods for dimensionality reduction of hyperspectral images. He has aspirations to incorporate advanced machine learning methods into the oceanographic community and being a community advocate for open source and open science.

In his free time, he enjoys learning Spanish, dancing and good company solving the societal problems of the world.
